Project Description
ReLocate An app me and some other designers created, ReLocate is a mobile app designed to help users easily find and book recreational facilities for various activities, such as sports and fitness sessions. It allows users to search for nearby centers, filter results based on preferences like activity type or availability, and view important details like schedules, facilities, and amenities. ReLocate also provides notifications and updates about activities, helping users stay informed and organized for their recreational plans. You can explore the prototype of ReLocate by following this link to the Figma prototype.
